Naran is a beautiful tourist site in Pakistan known for its stunning natural landscapes and picturesque views. It is located in the Kaghan Valley and offers activities like hiking, fishing, and exploring the nearby Saif-ul-Malook Lake.

The Army Stadium in Peshawar is a prominent sports venue in Pakistan. It hosts various sporting events, including cricket matches and football tournaments. The stadium has a vibrant atmosphere and can accommodate a large number of spectators. It's a great place to cheer for your favorite teams and enjoy thrilling sports action!

Garrison Park in Peshawar is a popular recreational spot for locals and tourists alike. It offers a serene environment with lush greenery, beautiful gardens, and walking trails. The park is a perfect place to relax, have a picnic, or enjoy outdoor activities with friends and family. Don't forget to capture some memorable moments amidst the natural beauty of Garrison Park
